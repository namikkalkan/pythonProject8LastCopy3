FAQ

How can I list a product?

Sign up our platform, upload photo of your item, decide the daily/hourly price of your item. Keep an eye for bookings now!!

How can I rent a product?

Search what / when you need, create an account in 2 mins pick it up from your neighbour

What happens if I damage the product?

Contact with our agent. If the item is unusable anymore we charge the 2.hand price of the item. If it has some issues we work with professionals to fix it in cheaper and quality way

I have problem with my booking / account.

Our agent will be there for you, call us or email us

Will my item be insured during hiring process?

Yes, we offer out of pocket options when you are renting the item

When will I get paid ?

Payments to our listers will be done at the first week of each month, if you need urgent no worries text us. We get you covered

What is the mission of rent for a day ?

Our vision is to be strong player of the market for a greener world,be part of less consuming environment and supporting each others.
